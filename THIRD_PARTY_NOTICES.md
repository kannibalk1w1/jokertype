## HyperType

JokerType uses HyperType as an implementation reference and includes its embedded `m6x11plus` pixel font data for the typing particle font. It also embeds the small `multhit1.mp3` and `gold_seal.mp3` sound assets for HyperType-style typing and enter sounds.
